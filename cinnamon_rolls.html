Cinnamon Rolls

By Alane Watkins, produced by Clawsons

Ingredients || Ингредиенты

  • Warm water: 2 1/2 cups || 550ml
  • Sugar: 1/2cup || 110gr
  • Yeast: 4Tbsp || 45gr
  • Eggs: 2
  • Salt: 1Tbsp || 20gr
  • Oil: 1/2 cup || 118ml
  • Flour: 7-8 cups || 1100-1209gr
  • Cinnamon: Spread over the entire surface || 15-20gr
  • Brown sugar: Spread over the entire surface || Распределить по всей поверхности
  • Dental floss(optional)

Frosting || Глазурь

  • Sour cream: 8 ounces || 227gr
  • Soft Butter: 2 sticks || 227gr
  • Vanilla sugar: 1Tbsp || 1 packet
  • Powdered sugar 4 cups || 436gr

Directions

Step 1 Make the dough

Combine warm water, sugar and yeast in a bowl. Mix everything together using a spoon. Let rise for 10 minutes. Add two eggs, salt and oil. Mix everything together. Add a little bit of flour at a time until all of it is added. Let the dough rest and double in size or put it in a warm place for about 30 min.

Step 2 Make the rolls

After the dough rises, separate it in half. Prepare a clean surface, put the flour on the surface, and add the dough. Roll dough into a rectangle. The dough should be half a centimeter or quarter ¼ of an inch thick.
Preheat the oven to 350F or 176C. Melt a little bit of butter and grease the top of the dough and put cinnamon and brown sugar over the entire surface. Roll up the dough like into a cylinder.
It is recommended to use dental floss to cut the cinnamon rolls. It is easier to cut them into even pieces, and they will not get smashed. Put the rolls on a pan and let them double in size for 30 min. Bake for 18-20 minutes and check them after 15 minutes. If golden brown, the rolls are ready.

Step 3 Frosting

In a bowl add 8 ounces of sour cream and 2 sticks of soft butter. Mix together until smooth. Add 1Tbsp of vanilla sugar and 4 cups of powdered sugar(little by little). Mix until smooth.

Step 4 Сompletion

As soon as the cinnamon rolls are baked, spread frosting on top. Enjoy!
