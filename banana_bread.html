Banana Bread

By Sister Nyland, produced by Nobody

Ingredients || Ингредиенты

  • Butter: 3/4c
  • Sugar: 2c
  • Eggs: 3
  • Bananas(ripe): 6
  • Sour Cream: 1 16oz
  • Vanilla: 2tsp
  • Cinnamon: 2tsp
  • Salt: 1/2tsp
  • Baking Soda: 3tsp
  • Flour: 4 1/2c
  • Chocolate Chips: 1/2 pkg
  • TEST
  • Warm water: 2 1/2 cups || 550ml
  • Sugar: 1/2cup || 110gr
  • Yeast: 4Tbsp || 45gr
  • Eggs: 2
  • Salt: 1Tbsp || 20gr
  • Oil: 1/2 cup || 118ml
  • Flour: 7-8 cups || 1100-1209gr
  • Cinnamon: Spread over the entire surface || 15-20gr
  • Brown sugar: Spread over the entire surface || Распределить по всей поверхности
  • Dental floss(optional)

Directions

Step 1

Preheat oven, 350F or 176C. Grease pan, sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar. Cream butter and sugar. Add eggs, mashed bananas, sour cream, vanilla, and cinnamon. Mix well. Add flour, salt, baking soda. Mix. Stir in chocolate chips.
